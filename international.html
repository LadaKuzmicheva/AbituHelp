1

We align programs with your goals, language level, budget, and timeline.

2

3

4

5

Visa & arrival

Typical requirements

The exact list depends on the university/program. Below is a common baseline used by many institutions.

Common documents

  • ✓Passport + photos (validity requirements vary)
  • ✓Education documents (diploma/transcript)
  • ✓Translations and legalization (as required)
  • ✓Application forms and consent documents

Medical & logistics

  • ✓Medical certificate pathway (often 086/U) — check per university
  • ✓HIV / HBV / HCV tests (common for student visa processes)
  • ✓Insurance and dormitory documents (varies)
  • ✓Migration registration steps after arrival

Don’t speak Russian?

Many universities offer a one-year foundation/preparatory course. It helps you reach the required language level and get ready for your degree program. We explain options and help you choose the right route in any region of Russia.

Tuition-free / scholarship / quota routes

International applicants may apply through government quota or scholarship programs. These routes typically require additional documents (motivation letter, recommendations, translations) and strict deadlines. We help you build the package logically and submit it correctly.
